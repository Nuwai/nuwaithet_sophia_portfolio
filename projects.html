Burmese Money Scam Detection

NLP and App

Built an end-to-end pipeline to identify scam-related Burmese messages (19K+ samples) with balanced performance and a public Streamlit app.

TF-IDF and SVM Class imbalance handling Streamlit deployment
  • Custom regex, POS-aware cleaning, and syllable tokenization for Burmese text.
  • Evaluation with ROC-AUC 0.97 and F1-score 0.89; threshold tuned for recall.
  • App enables public awareness and data collection for future retraining.

NLP

70K+ sentences XLM-R and BiLSTM-CRF FastAPI
